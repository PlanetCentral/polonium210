Introduction to Polonium-210

Polonium-210 (Po-210) is a highly radioactive element, naturally occurring through the decay of radon gas and uranium. Despite its rarity, it poses significant risks due to its extreme toxicity, even in microscopic amounts.

This element gained infamy for its use in high-profile poisonings but is also found in trace amounts in tobacco and industrial activities, making it a critical concern for public health.
